# 🏨 CRM — Customer Relationship Manager
### Your guests deserve more than an autoresponder.

If you've ever managed customer relations in hospitality, you already know:
every unanswered message is a missed booking, and every missed booking 
is a guest who chose someone else.

**CRM** is a fully local AI agent that handles every WhatsApp conversation 
your property receives — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — without anyone 
sitting at a desk waiting for the next notification.

It works in two modes, simultaneously:

**Proactive** — The moment a booking is confirmed in your PMS, the agent 
opens the conversation: welcome message, practical info, upsell opportunities, 
pre-arrival checklist, check-in reminder, post-stay follow-up. 
The full guest journey, automated.

**Reactive** — Every inbound WhatsApp message gets a response. 
Existing guests, curious visitors, people comparing prices before booking — 
the agent recognizes who it's talking to, loads the right context, 
and handles the conversation from first contact to confirmed reservation.

It connects directly to your PMS for real-time availability and pricing, 
builds personalized offers, manages FAQs, collects pre-arrival information, 
and knows exactly when to step aside and involve a human.

Everything runs inside your network. Guest data stays yours.
No cloud dependencies. No recurring API costs for the core logic.
Just a system that works quietly in the background while you focus 
on what actually matters — the hospitality.
